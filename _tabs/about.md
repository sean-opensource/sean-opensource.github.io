Chilepluto is a personal technical-notes site covering practical PowerShell,
Windows, Python, and local AI workflows.

The articles focus on repeatable steps, small working examples, and operational
notes that are useful when setting up or troubleshooting a tool.

## Topics

- PowerShell administration and automation
- Windows and WSL development environments
- Python examples for data and AI workflows
- Running open-source models locally
